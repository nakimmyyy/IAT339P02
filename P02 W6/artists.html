Master Artists

 

Vivienne

Vivienne has been with Minoko Nail Studio for over 6 years with over 10 years of certified nail designer, with a phenomenal background in Japanese 3D nail art.

“I am able to give each customer a unique individual nail experiences. One of my strongest points is combining the experiences I have gained in Japan and Canada to create new unique nail designs.”

 
 
 

Annie

Annie has been with Minoko Nail Studio for 6 years with over 15 years of certified nail designer. She is the Picasso of Nail Artists and is able to draw detailed nail art.

“I have been working as a nail designer since I was 16 years old. I worked as a nail artist in Korea for over 10 years and in Canada for 5 years. I gained an interest as a nail artist from my mother, who was also a nail designer in Korea. My strongest skillset is to customize detailed drawings for my customers.”

 
 
 

Crystal

Crystal has been with Minoko Nail Studio for 4 years with over 8 years of nail experience. Her strongest skillset is creating precise and unique French nail designs originating in Japanese nail culture.

“I am very passionate about my work. My goal is to make my clients look and feel their best! I find it very important to keep up with the latest trends, so I am constantly flying to Japan and other parts of Asia to learn about their nail culture.”

 
 
 

Junior Nail Artists

Minoko Nail Studio is constantly recruiting and teaching young artists on latest nail design skills. They are constantly in need of clients to practice their skills on. By selecting one of our junior nail artists, you will receive $10 off on all nail packages.
